Draw Rules

  • Each number costs £1 — you may select as many as you like.
  • Numbers are unique per venue. Once taken, a number cannot be selected again at that location.
  • Cash entries are recorded by venue staff. Card entries are processed securely via Stripe.
  • The draw takes place live on stage at the Diss Park Festival on 6th July 2026.
  • One winner will be drawn per venue. Each venue has its own hamper prize.
  • Winners must be present or contactable within 48 hours to claim their prize.
  • The draw is run by Diss & District Round Table 439. The organisers' decision is final.

How it works

1. Select your venue — choose the pub or location where you're entering.

2. Pick your lucky numbers — tap any available number from 1–100. Greyed out numbers are already taken at your venue.

3. Enter your details — a name and phone number so we can contact you if you win.

4. Pay — cash on the spot, or card via our secure Stripe link.

5. Come to the festival! — winners are drawn live on stage at the Diss Park Festival, 6th July 2026.

Privacy Policy

We only collect what's needed to run the draw — your name, phone number, venue, selected numbers, and payment method.

Your data is stored securely and is only accessible to Diss & District Round Table 439 committee members.

We will only use your phone number to contact you if you win. We will never share your data with third parties or use it for marketing.

All data will be deleted within 30 days after the draw closes on 6th July 2026.

About Round Table

Diss & District Round Table 439 is a local community organisation for men aged 18–45. We run events, raise money for good causes, and have a lot of fun doing it.

The BBQ Hamper Draw is one of several fundraisers we run throughout the year. All proceeds go directly to local charities and community projects in and around Diss.

The big reveal happens at the Diss Park Festival, 4–6 July 2026 — a free community event with live music, food, and entertainment. We'd love to see you there!
